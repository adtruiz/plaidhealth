Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Epic MyChart account, we access your health information through patient-authorized FHIR APIs. This includes demographics, lab results, medications, conditions, and appointment history.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your health data is displayed in the application interface for your personal use. This is a prototype application for testing FHIR integration capabilities.

3. Data Storage

Currently, authentication sessions are stored temporarily in server memory and are not persisted to a database. Access tokens expire after 1 hour.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal health information with third parties. Your data is accessed solely for displaying it back to you within the application.

5. Security

We use industry-standard HTTPS encryption for all data transmission. Access tokens are handled securely and are not logged or stored permanently.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to disconnect your Epic account at any time. You can revoke access through your Epic MyChart account settings.

7. HIPAA Compliance

This is a prototype application. For production use, we will implement full HIPAA compliance including encrypted storage, audit logging, and Business Associate Agreements.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
