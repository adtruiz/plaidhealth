Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2025

Important: VerziHealth is a personal project application designed to help you access and view your own health records in one unified location. We take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your health information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account when you sign in
  • Health Records: Medical records you authorize us to access from connected Electronic Health Record (EHR) systems including Epic, Cerner, and other FHIR-compatible providers
  • Connection Data: OAuth tokens and connection metadata needed to access your health records from authorized providers
  • Usage Information: Audit logs of when you access your data, for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the application
  • To retrieve and display your health records from connected EHR systems
  • To aggregate and present your health data in a unified view
  • To maintain security logs and detect unauthorized access
  • To improve the application's functionality and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All OAuth access tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption
  • Secure Transmission: All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS connections
  • Database Security: Health data is stored in a secure PostgreSQL database with access controls
  • Limited Access: Only you can access your health records through your authenticated account
  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, rent, or share your health information with third parties

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Account information is retained while your account is active
  • OAuth tokens and connection data are retained until you disconnect a provider
  • Audit logs are retained for 90 days for security purposes
  • You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View all health records we've retrieved on your behalf
  • Control: Connect or disconnect health record providers at any time
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Download your health data in standard formats
  • Revocation: Revoke OAuth access tokens at any time through connected providers

6. Third-Party Services

We integrate with the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth: For secure authentication (Google Privacy Policy applies)
  • EHR Providers: Epic, Cerner, and other FHIR-compatible systems (their privacy policies apply)
  • Railway: Cloud hosting platform (Railway Privacy Policy applies)

When you authorize access to your health records, you are subject to the privacy policies of those respective EHR providers.

7. HIPAA Compliance

This application is designed as a personal health record (PHR) tool. As a PHR, we are not directly subject to HIPAA regulations. However, we implement security practices consistent with HIPAA standards to protect your health information.

8. Data Breaches

In the event of a data breach affecting your personal health information, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of discovering the breach
  • Describe what information was compromised
  • Explain steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Provide guidance on protecting yourself
  • Notify relevant authorities as required by law

9. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. International Users

This application is hosted in the United States. All data is stored and processed in the United States. By using this application, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States.

11. De-Identified Information

We may create de-identified data from your health information for the following purposes:

  • Analytics: To understand usage patterns and improve the application
  • Research: To conduct healthcare research and develop new features
  • Quality Improvement: To assess and improve data quality and accuracy

De-identified data:

  • Has all personally identifiable information removed (name, email, dates of birth, etc.)
  • Cannot be used to identify you individually
  • May be aggregated with other de-identified data for statistical analysis
  • Is not subject to the same consent requirements as your personal health information

We follow industry-standard de-identification practices consistent with HIPAA Safe Harbor guidelines to ensure your privacy is protected.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you via email and display a prominent in-app notification when you next log in
  • Provide you with a summary of the changes made
  • Require you to review and re-affirm your consent before continuing to use the application
  • Give you the option to withdraw your consent and delete your account if you do not agree with the changes

You will have 30 days from the date of notification to review the updated policy and make your decision. If you do not respond within 30 days, your continued use of the application will be considered acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Consent Withdrawal

You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. When you withdraw consent:

  • Immediate Effect: We will immediately stop collecting new data from connected EHR providers
  • Access Revocation: All OAuth access tokens will be revoked and deleted
  • Data Options: You can choose to:
    • Delete All Data: Permanently delete your account and all associated health records from our systems
    • Export Then Delete: Download your health data before deletion
    • Retain for Legal Purposes: If required by law, we may retain de-identified audit logs for up to 90 days
  • Timeline: Data deletion is completed within 30 days of your request
  • Confirmation: You will receive email confirmation once deletion is complete

To withdraw consent, go to Account Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí "Withdraw Consent and Delete Account" or email privacy@stripehealthcare.com.

14. Change in Ownership or Business Closure

In the event of a change in ownership, merger, acquisition, or business closure:

Change in Ownership:

  • Advance Notice: We will notify you at least 60 days before any transfer of your data to a new owner
  • Your Options: You will have the option to:
    • Continue using the service under the new owner (subject to their privacy policy)
    • Export your data and delete your account before the transfer
    • Withdraw consent and have your data deleted
  • New Owner Obligations: Any new owner must:
    • Maintain the same or stronger privacy protections
    • Honor all existing consent agreements
    • Provide you with their updated privacy policy
  • No Transfer Without Notice: Your data will not be transferred without providing you adequate notice and options

Business Closure:

  • Advance Notice: We will provide at least 90 days notice before shutting down the application
  • Data Export Period: You will have at least 60 days to export your health data
  • Automatic Deletion: All personal health information will be permanently deleted within 30 days after service shutdown
  • Audit Logs: De-identified audit logs may be retained for legal compliance purposes only
  • OAuth Revocation: All OAuth tokens will be revoked and connection to EHR providers will be terminated
  • No Data Sale: Your personal health information will NOT be sold or transferred to any third party

Our Commitment: In any scenario involving ownership change or business closure, your privacy and data protection remain our highest priority. You will always have control over your health information.
